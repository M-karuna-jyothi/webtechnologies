INTRODUCTION ABOUT VIT:

Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) is a private higher education institute deemed to be university, and Institution of Eminence,[1] located in Vellore, Tamil Nadu, India. Founded in 1984, as Vellore Engineering College, by G. Viswanathan, the institution offers 20 undergraduate, 34 postgraduate, four integrated and four research programs. It has a second campus in Chennai.
